General Questions

How can I get a crop recommendation?

You can get a personalized crop recommendation by navigating to the "Crop Recommendations" section on your dashboard and entering your soil type, temperature, and rainfall data.

Is the fertilizer advice accurate?

Our fertilizer advice is based on data-driven insights and agricultural best practices. For best results, we recommend a physical soil test to supplement our recommendations.

What types of alerts will I receive?

You will receive real-time alerts for critical events such as weather changes, pest and disease outbreaks, and market price trends for your registered crops.

How do I contact an agricultural expert?

The chatbot can provide immediate assistance. For more detailed queries, you can use our feedback form to send a message, and an expert will get back to you.

Can I access the platform on my mobile phone?

Yes, the Eco-Farm Manager is a web-based platform designed to be fully responsive and accessible on all mobile devices.
